Four Consecutive Pucks is interesting strategy game for kids and adults. It has an educational value as it inspires the players to strategize and plan ahead. It improves the challenger minds and exercises their brain. It has different synonym names such as Connect Four, Captain's Mistress, Four Up, Plot Four, Find Four, Fourplay, Four in a Row and Four in a Line. It is a game for two players at the age range 7 and up.
The game consist of a grid of seven columns and six rows.

Each player has his won color to identify his discs. The main idea of the game is to make four consecutive discs even vertical, horizontal or diagonal.
